Onmac.net


As a spin off of the project, Colin created Onmac.net, a site with Forums, Wiki and a Blog to help the community of XP on Mac'ers get up and running with Windows on their Mac. With the support aspect of Bootcamp covered by Apple, and support for Parallels Desktop for Mac handled by Parallels, the role for Onmac.net was less relevant and the property was combined with InsanelyMac.com to add value for both Onmac.net users and InsanelyMac.com users.
