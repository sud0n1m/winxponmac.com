Parallels Desktop for Mac


The third entrant to the Windows on Mac arena was the one that most users were clamoring for. A solution that allows you to run Mac OS X and Windows XP simultaneously. Parallels took the world by storm with a very smart early public beta, much like Bootcamp. The Parallels team have been fast and furious at updating their software. With a beta of Vmware Fusion, the future of Parallels looks a little uncertain. However, if they can maintain their current pace, they have certainly earned the respect and hearts and minds of the Mac community - something Vmware has yet to do!
